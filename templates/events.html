9th-11th September
‘Hackonix', organized from 9th to 11th September 2022, was an inter-college hackathon in which students from some of the top-tier colleges of India participated. A workshop on Git and GitHub and a pre-event on stocks, ‘Stock Overflow,’ was also organized before the event. The hackathon consisted of four tracks- Educational Technology, Healthcare, Agriculture Technology, and Open Innovation. It tested students' problem-solving skills, giving them an insight into the real world. View Gallery >
